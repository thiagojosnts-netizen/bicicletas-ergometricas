**The 7 Criteria:**
1. **Distinctiveness** - Speech-stream visibility
2. **Brevity** - Four syllables or less
3. **Appropriateness** - Not too generic
4. **Easy Spelling/Pronunciation** - Accessible
5. **Likability** - Positive emotion
6. **Extendability** - Has "legs" for creative execution
7. **Protectability** - Can be trademarked

**6 Naming Styles:**
- Descriptive
- Suggestive
- Metaphorical
- Neological
- Historical
- Arbitrary
